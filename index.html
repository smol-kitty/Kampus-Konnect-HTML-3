University Health Centre (UHC) - The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda

The University Health Centre (UHC) at The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda has been serving as a trusted healthcare facility since 1964. Supported by the University Grants Commission, UHC is centrally located near the MSU Pavilion and Hansa Mehta Girls’ Hostel, making it easily accessible for students, faculty, and university staff.

UHC is dedicated to providing preventive, promotive, and curative healthcare services — all free of cost for MSU students and university employees' families. With a strong emphasis on scientific and rational treatment, the centre ensures quality care without financial burden. 💚

Key Services Offered

MSU Health Centre offers a wide range of essential and specialized medical services:

🔹 General Medical Consultations

🔹 Emergency & Outdoor Treatment

🔹Mental Health Support via MARG Counselling Centre (Since 1989)

🔹 Fully Equipped Diagnostic and Treatment Facilities

🔹 Specialized Consultations in:

  • Physiotherapy
  • Paediatrics
  • ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)
  • Orthopaedics
  • Dermatology

📌 All services are free for MSU students & Staff members
